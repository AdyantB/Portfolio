About School


G H Raisoni College of Engineering and Management Pune is one of the leading institutes in India, offering several programs 
in its management and engineering departments. The institute has highly qualified and accomplished faculty and staff that follow 
very interactive and innovative methods to teach students in effective and productive ways. The institute has received NAAC accreditation 
for having a good reputation among other colleges of its kind. The institute features many facilities such as library, hostels, mess,
placements, extracurriculars, etc that are available to students.

GH Raisoni College of Engineering and Management has a very dedicated and efficient placement cell which offers proper professional 
training and placement opportunities to students. In the annual year 2019, the institute saw a successful placement season with 97 
students from different departments getting placed across various sectors. The highest CTC offer during the placement session was worth 
 10 LPA and was made by TATA Projects Ltd. Other recruiting companies offering good packages to the students included Zensar 
 Technologies, Capgemini, TCS and Accenture.

GH Raisoni College of Engineering and Management offers several facilities and provisions on the campus to ensure that students receive 
best in class education and memorable experience during their college life:  

Labs: The state-of-the-art computer laboratory has a plethora of sections. And, each of it provides support to internet applications, 
design application and programming

Central Library: The students can visit the library which is well stocked to provide students with information and knowledge. The 
library features a nicely lit room that is well ventilated, well-structured books, journals and magazines

Gym: There is a well-equipped gym for students for health and fitness purposes available on the campus.

Hostel: Accommodation is provided to male and female students with all necessary facilities such as water supply, mess, a recreation 
room; 24/7 ambulance availability, sports facility, fitness center, and medical assistance for assuring the comfort of 
students.

Sports:  The campus features facilities for table tennis, chess, and carom as part of the indoor sports activities, and badminton, 
football, basketball, and cricket for outdoor sports. The well-trained coaches are a part of the institute faculty to guide, motivate 
and inspire the students.

Health Care: Health care professionals are employed in the institute to provide medical assistance.
